Brief Biography

         I'm currently a research assistant in the DS3Lab, Systems group, ETH Zürich, working with Prof. Ce Zhang.
        My research interests focus on distributed machine learning and in-database machine learning. Currently, I'm working on the Bugua system and openGauss-DB4AI project. I received my Master Degree from Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences and my Bachelor's Degree from Nankai University.
